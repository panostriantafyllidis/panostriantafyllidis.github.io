Hello,

My name is Panagiotis (Panos) Triantafyllidis. I am from Greece, and I came in the UK in 2018 for my undergraduate in BSc Business Computing and IT which I graduated with Honours. My curiosity and interactions I had thus far with this domain, had given me an interest to pursue a career in a CS/IT security-related role ( or at least learn how to do so in the process), ideally in Greece or in the UK.

I am currently looking for opportunities to start my career in the field of IT and Cyber Security.
